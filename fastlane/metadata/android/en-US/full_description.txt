ScreenOffTimer is a sleep timer for Android. No ads, just a simple, modern tool to help you wind down and protect your device's battery. life.

Features:
- Media Stop: Automatically stops music and media playback when the timer ends.
- Auto Lock Screen: Safely locks your device's screen once the countdown reaches zero.
- Return to Home: Automatically navigates back to the home screen for a clean state.
- Dark Theme Support: Seamlessly adapts to your system's theme for comfortable night-time use.
- Accessibility: This app requires the Accessibility Service permission solely to perform the Lock Screen action.
- Data Integrity: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information through the Accessibility Service.
